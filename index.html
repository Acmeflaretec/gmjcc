About Us

Great Ministry of Jesus Church

Great Ministry of Jesus Church was founded by Pastor Devadhanam, a visionary born and raised in a devoted missionary family. Inspired by his family's unwavering dedication to Jesus Christ, Pastor Devadhanam carries forward a legacy of bringing peace and joy to people struggling with life's challenges.

During his early years, Pastor Devadhanam experienced personal hardships and a period of darkness when he drifted away from a spiritual path. However, through a life-changing encounter with Jesus, he found his purpose and committed himself fully to Christ's mission.

Since then, he has been passionately working to spread the message of hope and redemption.
