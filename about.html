About Us

Building a Dignified Society

At GMJCC, our mission is to create a society where every individual can live with dignity and self-sustainability through compassion, education, and community service.


Founded by Pastor Devadhanam, GMJCC carries forward a legacy of spiritual and social transformation, inspired by unwavering faith and dedication to the teachings of Jesus Christ.


Our programs, from child sponsorship to agricultural development, are designed to empower communities, foster education, and promote sustainable living.


Located in Tirunelveli, our campus is easily accessible and serves as a hub for spiritual growth, education, and community initiatives.


Through our initiatives, we aim to spread hope, healing, and harmony across communities by addressing social and economic challenges with love and faith.

Our Programs

Transforming Communities

Explore the various programs at GMJCC, aimed at empowering individuals and building a better society.

Village Child Development Centre

In partnership with SPSK EduTech, we train teachers to provide quality education and life skills to children in rural villages.

Child Sponsorship Program

Through LYCM, this program provides education, healthcare, and nutrition to children in need, empowering their futures.

Mid-Day Meal Program

Offering free nutritious meals to individuals in need, supporting their health and well-being regardless of age or background.

Bible University

Providing in-depth theological education to prepare students for leadership roles in churches and communities.

Foreign Language College

In collaboration with Focus Academy, we offer courses in German, French, Italian, and Spanish for global opportunities.

Residential Education Home

Following the Genesis Educational System, this program nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and holistic growth in children.

Agricultural Development Centre

Promoting sustainable farming practices and supporting rural farmers through training and research.

Physical Education School

Encouraging youth to develop healthy lifestyles, teamwork, and leadership skills through sports and physical activities.

Medical College

Preparing healthcare professionals with quality education to serve communities and improve healthcare outcomes in Tirunelveli.

Why Choose Us

What Makes GMJCC Unique
